Improve the way your team deals with requests

When someone on your team is overloaded, or takes annual leave, incoming requests for applications, quotes, support, brochures etc are being locked inside their inbox, unaccessible by others.

This is exactly the situation you need to avoid.

Streamline your processes

Instead of pushing paper around the office, using email, or even Excel, Approwave handles incoming requests from staff & clients online.

So now, when a member of the team takes a holiday, or is struggling with their workload, the requests are easily picked up by another member of the team ensuring nothing falls between the cracks.
